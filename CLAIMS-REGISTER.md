# CLAIMS-REGISTER.md — per-page claim ledger

Every benefit/health/efficacy claim on the site, the page it appears on, the
claim-strength language used, and the specific cited study on that same page (BRIEF §9).
**To be read in full, not skimmed.** A qualified medical/scientific reviewer co-signs this
register before any production launch (BRIEF §18). Build date: 2026-07-14.

Legend — claim-strength language must match study design:
`MA` meta-analysis/systematic review · `RCT` randomized trial · `OBS` uncontrolled/observational ·
`MECH` mechanistic · `NULL` null/negative.
